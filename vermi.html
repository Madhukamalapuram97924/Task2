VermiCompost:

Vermicompost is a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer made from worm castings. It enriches soil, improves soil structure,
and enhances plant growth.To prepare vermicompost, organic waste like kitchen scraps and paper is fed to earthworms
in a controlled environment. The worms digest the waste, converting it into nutrient-packed compost.
It's eco-friendly, economical, and boosts soil health, making it ideal for gardening, farming, and landscaping.
1. Start with a suitable container like a bin with a lid.
2. Add bedding materials like shredded newspaper or cardboard to the container.
3. Introduce red worms (Eisenia fetida) to the bedding.
4. Layer kitchen scraps such as fruit and vegetable peels over the bedding.
5. Keep the bin moist, aerated, and in a dark, cool place, regularly adding more scraps as the worms break them
down into nutrient-rich vermicompost.
